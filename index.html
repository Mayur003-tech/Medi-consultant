About Us

"Navigating Your Medical Journey"

Welcome to Align Infotech , your premier destination for medical admission consultancy. We are dedicated to helping aspiring medical professionals navigate the complex and competitive world of medical school admissions. With our team of experienced consultants and personalized approach, we provide the guidance and support you need to achieve your dreams of a career in medicine.

Our Services

Inital consultation

Our comprehensive initial consultation assesses your academic background, extracurricular activities, and career aspirations. This helps us create a customized roadmap tailored to your unique profile and goals.

Learn More

Application Strategy

We assist you in crafting a compelling application that highlights your strengths and addresses any weaknesses. Our services include personal statement guidance, recommendation letter advice, and overall application enhancement to ensure you present your best self to admissions committees.

Learn More

Test Preparation

Align Infotech offers expert advice on preparing for standardized tests such as the MCAT. We provide access to study resources, practice tests, and effective preparation strategies to help you achieve competitive scores.

Learn More

Interview Coaching

Our interview coaching sessions simulate real medical school interviews, offering constructive feedback and helping you build the confidence and skills needed to impress admissions panels.

Learn More

Financial Aid and Scholarships

We guide you through the financial aspects of medical education, helping you explore available financial aid options, scholarships, and funding opportunities to make your education more affordable.

Learn More

Ongoing Support

Our support doesn't end with your application submission. We provide ongoing assistance throughout the admissions cycle, keeping you informed of updates, addressing any concerns, and celebrating your successes with you
